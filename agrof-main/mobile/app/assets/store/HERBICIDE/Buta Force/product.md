# Buta Force Herbicide

## Overview
Buta Force is a selective herbicide containing butachlor-based active ingredients, providing excellent control against annual grasses and some broadleaf weeds in various crops. This herbicide is highly effective for pre-emergence and early post-emergence weed management.

## Specifications
Active Ingredient: Butachlor-based active ingredients
Formulation: Emulsifiable Concentrate (EC)
Mode of Action: Selective herbicide
Target Weeds: Annual grasses, some broadleaf weeds
Application Areas: Rice, maize, soybeans, cotton crops
Packaging: 1L, 5L, 20L containers

## Usage Instructions
- **Mixing ratio**: 100-150ml per 20L of water
- **Application method**: Selective herbicide
- **Target weeds**: Annual grasses and some broadleaf weeds
- **Target crops**: Rice, maize, soybeans, cotton crops
- **Application timing**: Best applied pre-emergence or early post-emergence
- **Coverage**: Ensure thorough coverage of target weeds
- **Avoid drift**: Do not apply when windy to prevent drift
- **Re-entry period**: 12 hours after application

## Application Method
- **Method**: Foliar spray or soil treatment
- **Target**: Apply to weed foliage or soil surface
- **Equipment**: Boom sprayer for large areas, knapsack sprayer for small areas
- **Timing**: Pre-emergence or early post-emergence
- **Selective control**: Targets weeds while protecting crops

## Benefits
- **Grass weed control**: Effective control of annual grasses and some broadleaf weeds
- **Selective action**: Kills grass weeds, spares crops
- **Dual application**: Pre-emergence and early post-emergence options
- **Multi-crop use**: Suitable for rice, maize, soybeans, cotton crops
- **Cost-effective**: Reduces manual weeding costs
- **Time saving**: Quick and efficient application
- **Easy application**: Simple mixing and spray application
