## Usage Instructions
- **Variety type**: E107 sesame variety is a mechanizable high yielding, non shattering and drought resistant sesame variety
- **Maturity period**: Matures in 3 months
- **Planting method**: Preferably planting in line or can be broadcasted after clear guidelines from an agronomist
- **Row spacing**: Row to row spacing of 15cm and continuous drilling
- **Planting ratio**: Mixing ratio 2.5 kgs of seed to 8kgs of sand and ash for 1 acre
- **Fertilization**: For maximum yields, Use of fertilizers is encouraged
- **Yield potential**: Yields up to 5 bags/ acre
- **Planting depth**: 1-2 cm deep
- **Planting time**: After last frost, when soil temperature is 20°C+
