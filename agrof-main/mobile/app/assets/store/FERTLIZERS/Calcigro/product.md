# Calcigro

## Supplier
Agritab Uganda Limited

## Overview
CalciGrow is a dust-free ultrafine granulated calcium mineral fertilizer that is designed to rapidly neutralize acidic soils. By doing so, it maximizes nutrient availability, optimizes NPK fertilizer and micronutrient uptake, increases moisture retention, seed germination, and calcium for plant growth.

## Key Benefits
- High calcium availability for better nutrient absorption
- Strengthens plant structure and improves crop quality
- Reduces common calcium deficiency symptoms
- Compatible with most foliar spray programs

## Usage Instructions
- **Application timing**: Apply before planting or during soil preparation
- **Method**: Broadcast evenly and incorporate into soil
- **Application rate**: 1-2 tons per acre depending on soil acidity
- **Soil conditions**: Most effective in acidic soils (pH below 6.0)
- **Timing**: Apply 2-4 weeks before planting for best results
- **Water management**: Water after application to help dissolution

## Benefits
- **Soil pH correction**: Rapidly neutralizes acidic soils
- **Nutrient availability**: Maximizes availability of NPK and micronutrients
- **Moisture retention**: Improves soil water holding capacity
- **Seed germination**: Enhances seed germination rates
- **Plant structure**: Strengthens plant cell walls and structure
- **Crop quality**: Improves overall crop quality and yield
- **Long-term benefits**: Provides lasting soil improvement
