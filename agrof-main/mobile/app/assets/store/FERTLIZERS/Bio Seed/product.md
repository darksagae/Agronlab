# Bio Seed

## Supplier
Ag-ploutos Company Limited

## Overview
This is a new, all-natural way to inoculate crops and soils with beneficial bacteria and fungi that work to: Fix atmospheric nitrogen for plant use, solubilize phosphorous from soil, which is often unavailable, Mobilize potassium for easier plant uptake, solubilize silicon in soil, which will improve drought resistance, expand and colonize the root zone for increased nutrient uptake. Bio Seed is safe to mix with liquid fertilizers and even with in-furrow fungicides or insecticide application. Drip irrigation or root drenches on established orchards are also effective application methods.

## Application
- **In furrows**: Mix 50g (2 scoops) of Bio Seed per acre planted
- **Applied as seed coating**: determine amount for treatment as 0.3% of seed weight, Mix 1g of Bio Seed per 6 ml of water and apply to seeds, apply evenly to seeds and dry in shed, plant when dry
- **Dipping transplants**: Mix 50g (2 scoops) of Bio Seed per acre, soak roots thoroughly
- **Drenching orchards or dipping newly transplanted orchards**: Mix 100g (4 scoops) per acre planted, thoroughly drench base of tree or soak through root ball before planting. Re-apply once per season
- **Drip Irrigation**: Apply 50g (2 scoops) per acre through drip line twice per season
