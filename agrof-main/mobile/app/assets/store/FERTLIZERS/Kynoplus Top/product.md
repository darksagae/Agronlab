## Overview
Contains N-46% Slow release Nitrogen (Blue Urea). It is a number one product among different N-hanced Nitrogen fertilizers with a higher mount of Nitrogen. It is coated with AGROTAIN which prevents Nitrogen losses through volatilization hence increasing available Nitrogen in the soil. It reduces Plant stunts and enhances early growth stage of the plant. The use of Kynoplus Top improves the efficiency & effectiveness of applies Nitrogen, which results in improved crop quality, yield and profit. Application rate: 50-100kg per acre depending on crop.
