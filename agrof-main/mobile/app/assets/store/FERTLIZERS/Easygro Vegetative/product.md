# Easygro Vegetative

## Supplier
Osho Chemical Industries Ltd

## Overview
This is a fertilizer with chelated micro-elements and bio-stimulants for foliar feeding and fertigation. It is recommended for use during: Plant growth periods that require relatively high Nitrogen levels e.g. during periods of vegetative growth, Directly after harvest when nutrients need to be stored for the next season. It is ideal for the vegetative growth stage of crops (e.g., shortly after establishment) as it enhances formation of adequate foliage for photosynthesis. Due to its high Nitrogen content, plants have healthy vigorous growth, strong root development, dark green foliage, increased seed/fruit formation and higher yields.

## Composition
N.P.K 27: 10: 16 plus Trace Elements (S, Mg, Zn, Fe, Mn, Cu, B, Mo) Plus bio stimulants

## Dosage
- **All vegetables**: 30-40grams per 20ltrs of water
- **Fruit trees**: 80grams/20ltrs of water

## Usage Instructions
- **Application method**: Foliar spray or fertigation
- **Timing**: Apply during vegetative growth stages and after harvest
- **Frequency**: Every 2-3 weeks during active growth
- **Best time**: Early morning or late afternoon
- **Target stages**: Vegetative growth, shortly after establishment, post-harvest
- **Dosage**: Follow specific rates for vegetables and fruit trees
- **Nutrient storage**: Apply after harvest for next season storage

## Benefits
- **High nitrogen content**: Promotes vigorous vegetative growth
- **Foliage development**: Enhances formation of adequate foliage for photosynthesis
- **Root development**: Promotes strong root development
- **Dark green foliage**: Improves leaf color and health
- **Fruit formation**: Increases seed/fruit formation potential
- **Higher yields**: Significantly increases crop productivity
- **Chelated elements**: Better absorption of trace elements and biostimulants
