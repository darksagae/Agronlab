# Cassava Tapiocal- Microfood Cassava Foliar

## Supplier
Bukoola Chemical Industries (U) Ltd

## Overview
To use Cassava Tapioca (Microfood Cassava Foliar), dilute according to the instructions on the label. Apply the foliar spray directly onto cassava leaves every 2-3 weeks during the active growing season. Ensure full leaf coverage for effective absorption. For best results, spray early in the morning or late afternoon to avoid evaporation. Repeat applications during periods of stress or nutrient deficiency. Always follow recommended dosage to avoid over-application.
