## Overview
Muriate of Potash or Potassium Chloride (K 60%). Potash is primarily used to support plant growth, increase crop yield. It stimulates the growth of strong stems and gives the plant disease resistance by promoting thickness of the outer cell walls & can reduce moisture loss from growing plants, thereby giving some drought resistance.
