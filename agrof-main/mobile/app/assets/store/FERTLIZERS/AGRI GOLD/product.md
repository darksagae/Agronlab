# Agri Gold Foliar Fertilizer

## Overview
Agri Gold is a wonder product that prevents flower shedding, promotes more flower formation and bumper yield while enhancing healthy fruit formation and vegetative growth. Agri Gold is recommended in all flowering crops like tomatoes, peppers, beans, cotton, coffee. Agri Gold is approved for use in organic Agriculture.

## Application/Usage
- **Foliar application**: Mix 2-3mls of Agri Gold per 1ltr of water (60mls/20ltrs)
- **Drip Irrigation**: Mix 5mls of Agri Gold per 1ltr of water

## Usage Instructions
- **Application timing**: Apply during flowering and fruit development stages
- **Frequency**: Apply every 2-3 weeks during active growth
- **Best time**: Early morning or late afternoon to avoid evaporation
- **Coverage**: Ensure complete leaf coverage for maximum absorption
- **Compatibility**: Can be mixed with most pesticides and fungicides
- **Storage**: Keep in cool, dry place away from direct sunlight
