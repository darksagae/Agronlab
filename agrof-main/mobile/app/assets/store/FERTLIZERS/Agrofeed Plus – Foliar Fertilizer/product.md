# Agrofeed Plus – Foliar Fertilizer

## Supplier
Osho Chemical Industries Ltd

## Overview
AGROFEED PL is a liquid foliar fertilizer and plant booster, which contains well-balanced nutrients essential for plant growth. It is especially formulated for rapid and effective absorption through leaves. AGROFEED may also be applied through irrigation systems such as drip lines, overhead systems. It contains NPK: 12:10:8 + TE + 2% MgO+4%S+Amino Acids + Biostimulants +Growth Hormones + 2% CaO.

## Usage Instructions
- **Application method**: Foliar spray or through irrigation systems
- **Timing**: Apply during active growth stages and stress periods
- **Frequency**: Every 10-14 days during growing season
- **Best time**: Early morning or late afternoon
- **Coverage**: Ensure complete leaf coverage
- **Mixing**: Compatible with most pesticides and fungicides
- **Drip irrigation**: Can be applied through drip lines and overhead systems
